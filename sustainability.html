Sustainability

Investing
with Conscience

Sustainability at Aflak Holdings is not a reporting exercise. It is a lens through which we evaluate every investment decision, every partnership, and every commitment we make.

As a new company, we have the rare opportunity to embed sustainability from the very beginning — not to retrofit it onto an existing portfolio, but to make it a founding condition of how we invest and operate.

We are committed to aligning with the UAE's Net Zero 2050 vision and the broader ESG principles that define responsible capital stewardship in the 21st century.

Our ESG Framework

Three Pillars of
Responsible Investment

Environmental

We evaluate the environmental footprint of every asset we consider. In real estate, this means energy efficiency standards, green building certifications, and sustainable materials. In technology, it means backing platforms that reduce, not increase, resource consumption. We will not invest in sectors or assets whose environmental impact is irreconcilable with long-term sustainability.

Social

We believe that capital deployed responsibly creates social as well as financial returns. This means investing in assets and businesses that contribute positively to the communities they operate within — creating employment, supporting financial inclusion, and treating all stakeholders with dignity and fairness. It also means how we treat our own people: with respect, transparency, and genuine care for their development.

Governance

Governance is where sustainability meets accountability. We operate within the DFSA regulatory framework — one of the world's most demanding — and we embrace that standard as a baseline, not a ceiling. Our internal governance structures are designed to ensure that every decision is properly authorised, properly documented, and properly reviewed.

Our Commitments

What We Commit
to From Day One

01

ESG Due Diligence on Every Investment

We integrate environmental, social, and governance screening into our investment evaluation process from the earliest stage of any opportunity assessment. ESG is not a post-investment consideration — it is a pre-condition of investment.

02

Transparent Reporting to Stakeholders

We will publish annual sustainability disclosures alongside our financial reports, providing a clear and honest account of how we have lived up to our stated principles. We hold ourselves to the same transparency we demand of others.

03

Preference for Green-Certified Real Estate

Within our real estate sector, we will actively prioritise assets that meet or are being developed to meet recognised green building standards such as LEED or BREEAM, consistent with the UAE's vision for a sustainable built environment.

04

Technology That Enables, Not Extracts

In our technology sector investments, we favour platforms that create genuine value for their users — that improve access to financial services, reduce friction, or democratise opportunity — over those that simply extract margin without creating corresponding benefit.

UAE Alignment

Aligned with the
UAE's Vision

In Step with
Net Zero 2050

The UAE has made ambitious and genuine commitments to sustainability — from the Net Zero by 2050 strategic initiative to hosting COP28 in Dubai. As a company headquartered in the UAE and investing primarily in the region, Aflak Holdings is committed to operating in a manner consistent with these national goals.

We see the UAE's sustainability agenda not as a constraint but as a directional signal — pointing toward the sectors, assets, and business models that will create the most durable value over the coming decades.

Net Zero 2050 We align our investment strategy with the UAE's commitment to achieving net zero carbon emissions by 2050.
Vision 2031 Our technology and financial services investments support the UAE's ambition to build a knowledge-based, innovation-driven economy.
DIFC Standards As a DIFC-based entity, we operate under one of the world's leading financial regulatory frameworks, consistent with global sustainability standards.
Social Value We support the UAE's commitment to inclusive economic growth, financial literacy, and equitable access to capital across the community.
