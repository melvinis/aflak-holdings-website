Our Approach

Owning Assets That
Define Markets

Real estate is the most tangible of investment classes. Done well, it creates places where people live, work, and build their lives. Done poorly, it generates short-term returns at the cost of long-term value. At Aflak Holdings, we intend to do it well.

We focus on premium assets — residential developments in sought-after locations, commercial properties with quality tenants, and mixed-use projects that serve the evolving needs of a growing, cosmopolitan population. We are not traders. We are owners. And we approach every acquisition with the mindset of a long-term steward, not a short-term speculator.

Our real estate activity is currently in the pipeline and due diligence phase. We are evaluating a focused set of opportunities consistent with our investment criteria and will announce activity as investments are formalised.

Investment Criteria

What We Look
for in an Asset

LocationPrime or emerging prime locations with strong connectivity, established infrastructure, and clear long-term demand drivers. We focus primarily on Dubai, with selective interest in Abu Dhabi and key GCC markets.
QualityAssets built or being built to the highest construction and design standards, with specifications that attract and retain quality tenants or owners. We do not compromise on build quality.
SustainabilityA preference for assets that meet or are being developed to meet recognised green building standards, consistent with our ESG commitments and the UAE's Net Zero 2050 agenda.
TenureA minimum intended hold period of 5 years. We are buyers of assets we intend to own through cycles, not traders seeking short-term value extraction.
Return ProfileRisk-adjusted returns commensurate with the quality and duration of the investment. We do not chase yield at the expense of capital preservation or asset quality.
