Who We Are

Built on Expertise,
Guided by Integrity

Aflak Holdings is a Dubai-based investment group established to identify, structure, and steward capital across three sectors: real estate, financial services, and technology. We are founded by two professionals whose combined careers span over four decades across some of the most respected institutions in the UAE and internationally.

Our founders bring complementary strengths — one deeply rooted in HNW client relationship management and banking, the other in financial technology, data analytics, and digital platform architecture. Together, they bring both the relationship depth and the analytical rigour that serious investment demands.

We operate from the DIFC, Dubai's premier financial centre, within a regulatory environment that demands the highest standards of governance, transparency, and client protection. These standards are not constraints we navigate — they are principles we embrace.
