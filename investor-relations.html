At Aflak Holdings, investor relations is not a department — it is a disposition. We believe that the people who entrust us with their capital deserve clear, honest, and timely communication at all times.

As a newly established group, we are laying the groundwork for an investor relations programme that reflects the highest standards of disclosure and stakeholder communication. Our commitment to transparency begins before we have assets to report — because the culture of disclosure starts at founding, not at scale.

Our Commitment
to Stakeholders

We will communicate proactively and honestly with all stakeholders — not just when things are going well, but especially when they are not. We will publish our annual financial statements within 90 days of each financial year end. We will respond to investor enquiries within two business days.

These are not aspirations. They are commitments.
